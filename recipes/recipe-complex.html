Caramel Slice

Amy's famous caramel slice. A big hit at birthdays and Christmas.

  • 180C
  • 60 minutes
  • Sweet
  • Slice
  • Chocolate
  • Christmas

Ingredients

  • Biscuit Base
  • 1 cup self-raising flour
  • 1 cup desicated coconut
  • 1/2 cup caster sugar
  • 125g butter, melted
  • Caramel Filling
  • 400g can sweetened condensed milk
  • 20g butter
  • 2 tbspn golden syrup
  • Chocolate Topping
  • 150g dark chocolate
  • 20g copha

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C, line and lightly grease a shallow tin with baking paper, overhanging on opposite sides
  2. Sift flour into bowl. Mix in coconut and sugar and stir through the melted butter. Press firmly into tin and bake for 12-15 minutes until lightly coloured. Allow to cool.
  3. Place ingredients for caramel filling into small saucepan. Bring to the boil, stirring constantly. Then boil for 4-5 minutes until lightly caramelised. Quickly pour over base. Bake for 10 minutes and cool.
  4. Melt chocolate and copha with a heatproof bowl over water. Pour over caramel. Refrigerate for 20 minutes. Cut into pieces with a hot dry knife.
